The target audience or users of the Language Moderator are kids, teens and adults playing MMO games that have group chat features.
Since most MMOs and chatrooms have voice chat options, the conversations can be automatically monitored to provide users with a more friendly environment.

According to a poll conducted by YouGov 63% of adults use group chat regularily.
Games such as Fortnite and League of Legends have recently added voice chat option for players to communicate, following the trend of MMO gaming.
