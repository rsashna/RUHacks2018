The Language Moderator in Game Applications
    About
    Target Audience
    Applications


This app would be ideal for use in mutiplayer games with voice chat such as Team Fortress 2, Fortnite, Overwatch, DOTA 2, World of Warcraft, DCUO, PUBG etc.

Language Moderators would also be useful for group chat applications such as Discord, Blizard Voice Chat, mummble, Team Speak, Hangouts, Slack etc. since they are often used for gammers to communicate while playing games.

Incorporating the chat moderator into these games and applications would reduce the amount of foul language used during gamer interaction thus reducing cyberbullying
